Amplifying the clinical team in the patient's home

By continuously engaging, assessing, and connecting primary care providers to their older adult patients, ElliQ helps deliver better quality care and a better patient experience within the home

Customizing ElliQ

The patient’s experience with ElliQ is fully customizable. ElliQ is configured with which questions to ask, how often, what to track and observe (e.g. pain levels, medication intake), and more.

Engagement with ElliQ

ElliQ proactively engages the patient in conversations and activities throughout the day, and sends collected data directly to the primary care provider.

Actionable data and insights

Primary care providers gain insights on patients’ longitudinal observed and self-reported data and patterns. ElliQ will detect anomalies and notify the clinical team in real time.
