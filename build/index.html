Improving lives with empathetic digital companions

Our empathetic digital companions establish long-term relationships between people and technology, unlocking unprecedented continuous engagement. We’re using this breakthrough to help people live better, starting with older adults.

It all starts with empathy

Technology is woven into our lives, with the purpose of assisting us. Yet it’s designed to be utilitarian in value, without a genuine relationship being formed. To truly deliver on our need for connection and drive deep engagement, technology needs the ability to project empathy.

Fusing deep tech with psychology

After years of research and user studies combining psychology, behavioral sciences, and advanced cognitive AI capabilities, we built the first-ever empathetic digital companion agent — one that delivers on our needs, while projecting empathy to deliver engagement, trust, and collaboration between the agent and the user.
