Empathetic digital Companions

We’re harnessing the power of empathy to create long-term user engagement

Improving lives with empathetic digital companions tm

Our empathetic digital companions establish long-term relationships between people and technology, unlocking unprecedented continuous engagement. We’re using this breakthrough to help people live better, starting with older adults.

Healthcare

It all starts with empathy

Technology is woven into our lives to assist us. Yet it’s utilitarian and doesn't form a true relationship with us. To satisfy our need for connection and engagement, technology needs to project empathy.

Fusing technology with psychology

After years of research and user studies combining psychology, behavioral sciences, and advanced cognitive AI capabilities, we built the first-ever empathetic digital companion — one that delivers our needs while projecting empathy and gently encouraging engagement, trust, and collaboration between the companion and the user.

Powered by Cognitive AI technology

Proactive

Proactive

Initiates the right interaction, at the right time, in the right manner, based on a set of dynamic user and agent goals.

Context-aware

Context-aware

Uses multiple sensory and historic data points to construct a fused, dynamic state, in order to estimate availability to interact, emotional state, and to construct a contextual conversation.

Personalized

Personalized

Remembers key user information and uses it together with contextual bandit-based methods to choose the best user experience in order to increase the probability of successful user engagement.

Goal-based decision making

Goal-based decision making

Plans and executes its behavior to fulfill specific goals and KPIs that are set by experts and the user.

Learning

Learning

Constructs a predictive model of the user’s behavior, based on historic user and population data.

Multimodal

Multimodal

Expresses itself through multiple modalities and conversational design variants that are curated to feel natural and familiar to the user.

Evolving

Evolving

Content-rich experiences that aren’t anticipated by the user, enabled by engineering free tools for rapid content updates.
