Better care starts with empathy

Continuously engage, assess, and empower older adults with ElliQ.

Clinical teams need a glimpse into the home

Engaging older adults at home is tough. There are major gaps in communication, and primary care providers have no idea what’s really happening within the home, outside of visits together.

Introducing ElliQ: The sidekick for healthier aging

ElliQ, by Intuition Robotics, is a personalized care companion that extends the clinical team’s presence into older adults’ homes — helping improve their experience and increase patient engagement, while offering actionable data, notifications, and insights for early detection and intervention.

Empowering patients and primary care providers

ElliQ supports older adults’ independence, providing them with social companionship, wellness activities, connection to various care circles (i.e. family members and caregivers), and the ability to take charge of their health. ElliQ also tracks patients’ activity within the home, and offers a direct channel of communication between the primary care provider and the patient.

Amplifying the clinical team in the patient's home

By continuously engaging, assessing, and connecting primary care providers to their older adult patients, ElliQ helps deliver better quality care and a better patient experience within the home

Customizing ElliQ

The patient’s experience with ElliQ is fully customizable. ElliQ is configured with which questions to ask, how often, what to track and observe (e.g. pain levels, medication intake), and more.

Engagement with ElliQ

ElliQ proactively engages the patient in conversations and activities throughout the day, and sends collected data directly to the primary care provider.

Actionable data and insights

Primary care providers gain insights on patients’ longitudinal observed and self-reported data and patterns. ElliQ will detect anomalies and notify the clinical team in real time.
